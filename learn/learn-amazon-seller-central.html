Understanding Performance Metrics

Performance Metrics in Amazon Seller Central are key indicators of your store’s operational efficiency. They help Amazon – and you – monitor the quality of service you provide to your customers. Some vital metrics to watch include:

  • Order Defect Rate (ODR):
    This calculates the percentage of orders that have received anegative feedback, an A-to-Z Guarantee claim or a service chargeback.
  • Pre-fulfillment Cancel Rate (PCR):
    The percentage of orders canceled by the seller before shipment confirmation.
  • Late Shipment Rate (LSR):
    The percentage of orders shipped late by the seller.

Understanding these metrics and working towards improving them can significantly enhance your store’s performance and reputation.
